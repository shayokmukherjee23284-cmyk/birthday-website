For the girl with a soft heart.

Nandika is the kind of person whose presence feels gentle even before she says much. She feels deeply, cares sincerely, and carries a heart that still chooses kindness even when life gives her reasons to become harder.

There is something rare about her. The cute smile, the emotional reactions, the way she overthinks tiny things because they matter to her, and the quiet strength behind all of it. She is soft, but not weak. She is emotional, but not careless. She is innocent, but full of values.

Her dream of making her parents proud says everything about her heart. She wants to become someone they can look at and say, “She did it.” And maybe she does not realize it enough, but people like her make the world warmer just by existing.
